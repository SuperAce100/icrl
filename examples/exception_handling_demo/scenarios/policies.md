# ACME Corp Official Policies

> These policies are binding and must be followed by all customer-facing staff.

## Refund Policy

- Refunds are available within **30 days** of purchase
- After 30 days, no refunds will be issued
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Partial refunds are not available

## Subscription & Billing

- All subscriptions are billed monthly or annually in advance
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
- No pro-rated refunds for early cancellation
- Failed payments result in service suspension after 7 days

## Contract Terms

- All customers must agree to our standard Terms of Service
- Custom contract terms are not available
- Enterprise agreements use the standard Enterprise ToS
- No modifications to liability, indemnification, or data processing terms

## Account Access

- Password resets require email verification
- Account recovery requires government-issued ID
- Locked accounts require 24-hour security review
- No exceptions to security protocols

## Support Response Times

- Free tier: 48-hour response time
- Pro tier: 24-hour response time
- Enterprise tier: 4-hour response time
- Critical issues: Best effort for all tiers

## Data & Privacy

- Data deletion requests processed within 30 days (GDPR/CCPA)
- Data exports available in CSV format only
- No access to raw database records
- Audit logs retained for 90 days

## Discounts & Promotions

- Promotional discounts cannot be combined
- Discounts are not applied retroactively
- Annual plans receive 20% discount (standard)
- No additional discounts available

## Escalation Policy

- All escalations go through the support queue
- No direct escalation to executives
- Escalations are reviewed within 48 hours
